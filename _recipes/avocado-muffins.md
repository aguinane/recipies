ingredients:
- 3 cups plain flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp baking powder
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup margarine 
- 2 ripe avocados
- 2 eggs 
- 1 cup milk
- 2 tbsp lemon juice

directions:
- Preheat oven to 200 degrees
- Grease muffins trays
- In bowl sift flour, baking powder, salt and sugar
- Add butter and rub with finertips into dry ingredients until crumbly
- Blend avocado, eggs, milk and lemon juice
- Add wet ingredients to bowl, slowly stiring
- Spoon mixture into muffins tray
- Bake for 25-30 mins
- Allow to cool on wire rack for 10 mins prior to eating
